We are team of that studies vegetable oils and immune signals.

Get Started

About Us

The laboratory was established in 2021, with complete instruments and an area of about 200 square meters.

Mainly taking Arabidopsis thaliana, poplar and Carya illinoensis as the research objects, doing the work related to oil synthesis and plant immunity. The research directions are as follows:

  • Regulation of plant oil metabolism.
  • Study on plant disease resistance signal with oil participation.
  • Development of small molecules with plant immune activity.
  • Study on functional genes of forest trees.

Research methods include molecular biology, biochemistry, genetics and chemical genetics.
